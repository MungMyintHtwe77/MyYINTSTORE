Frequently Asked Questions

Do you provide warranty on products?

Yes, all our products come with official manufacturer warranty. Smartphones typically have 1-2 year warranty, while accessories have 6-12 months warranty.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards, bank transfers, mobile banking, and popular e-wallet services including TrueMoney and PromptPay.

How long does shipping take?

Standard shipping takes 2-3 business days within Bangkok and 3-5 business days for other provinces. Express shipping is available for next-day delivery.

Can I return or exchange products?

Yes, we offer 30-day return policy for unopened items in original packaging. Opened items can be exchanged within 7 days if defective.
